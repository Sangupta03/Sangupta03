### 🧭 The short story

Every project I build starts with the same question: **"why is a human still doing this?"**

- Watching my family juggle ten tabs to plan one trip → I built **five AI agents that negotiate the itinerary for them** ✈️
- Seeing engineers scroll 50,000-line log files at 2 AM → I built **ML that reads the logs and tells you what broke, in plain English** 📜
- Drowning in wall-of-text system design articles → I built **an AI tutor that draws the architecture diagram for you** 🏗️

I'm a B.Tech CSE (AI & ML) student at **VIT Bhopal** *(CGPA 9.06)*. This summer I interned at the **State Bank of India** (May–June 2026), building an ML-powered credit-risk platform — XGBoost scoring, microservices, and Gemini-generated credit memos.

My rule: **no project is finished until it's deployed and someone else can click a link and use it** — all three of my flagship AI projects are live right now.
